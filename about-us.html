About Us

Tycol is a Nigerian indigenous engineering company established for the purposes of providing quality and efficient Engineering, technical, procurement and construction services in the

  • upstream and downstream Oil and Gas sector and
  • Manufacturing and Power industries

as well as the different arms of the Nigerian Government in its developmental initiatives. The company was incorporated in 1968 with Registration Number RC 90068

How We Operate

Our operation ensures a sublime, effective and quality completion of EVERY project we undertake. We combine a healthy and safety oriented work pattern of best standards and practices. We adopt the best engineering ethics and quality control methods to give our customers the desired satisfaction. The component values guiding our work include

  • Creditable Integrity
  • Quality Assurance
  • Effective Teamwork
  • Safety
